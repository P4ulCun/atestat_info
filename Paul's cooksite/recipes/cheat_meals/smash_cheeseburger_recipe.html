The Ultimate Smash Cheeseburger

Ingredients

  • Hamburger bun - 1 (buttered and toasted)

  • Ground beef - 2 balls of 55g each

  • American cheese - 1 slice

  • Oil

  • Salt

  • Pepper

  • Sweet pickle relish - 1 teaspoon

     

  • Ketchup - 2 tablespoons

  • Mayonnaise - 2 tablespoons

  • Vinegar - a splash

  • Lettuce

  • Onion - sliced

  • Tomato - sliced

  • Pickles

Steps

  1. To make the Thoussand Island Dressing combine in a bowl the ketchup, mayo, vinegar

    and sweet pikle relish.

  2. Prepare the burger bun by laying the toppings on the bottom half of it.

  3. Heat up the oil in a skillet, then place the beef balls and smash them down with a metal spatula,

    using a second spatula to add pressure.

  4. Season generously with salt and pepper and cook until the patties are well browned (about 45 seconds).

  5. Carefully scrape the patties from the pan, making sure to get all of the browned bits.

  6. Flip the patties and immediately place a slice of cheese over 1 patty, then stack the second one

    directly on top. Immediately remove from the pan and transfer to the waiting burger bun.

Enjoy!
