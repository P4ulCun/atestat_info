Easy chicken curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast/thighs - 700g

  • Olive oil - 2 tablespoons

  • Onion - 1

  • Garlic - 5 cloves

  • Tomato sauce - 400g

  • Spinach - 60 g

  • Cooking cream - a splash

     

  • Salt

  • Pepper

  • Paprika

  • Ground cumin

  • Oregano

  • Basil

  • Chilli flakes

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into cubes and add all the aromatics.

  2. Heat up the oil in a skillet and sauté the garlic and onion.

  3. Add the cubed chicken to the and cook for about 5 minutes, turning occasionally.

  4. Add the tomato sauce and continue stirring.

  5. Add the spinach and cook until softened, then add a splash of cooking cream.

  6. Serve with a side of rice and

Bon Appétit!
