Burst cherry tomato pasta - easy and delicious recipe that’s ready in 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti - 100g

  • Cherry tomatoes - 250g

  • Garlic - 4 cloves

  • Olive oil - 1 tablespoon

  • Parmesan - to sprinkle on top

 

  • Salt

  • Chilli flakes

  • Basil

  • Oregano

  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta for about 12 minutes or until al dente.

  2. In a pan, heat up the oil and sauté the chopped garlic until they almost turn brown,

    then add the tomatoes.

  3.  After a few minutes, the skins on some will start to split. When this happens, use a wooden spoon

    and smash the tomatoes one by one to form the sauce.

  4. Add all the aromatics and cook for a few minutes.

  5. Strain the pasta (remember to keep some of the pasta water).

  6. Add it to the tomato sauce and mix vigurously adding some pasta water if needed.

Enjoy!
