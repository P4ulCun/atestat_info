The classic cookies

Ingredients

  • Flour - 450g

  • Baking soda - 1 tablespoon

  • Salt - 2 pinches

  • Sugar - 200g

  • Brown sugar - 200g

  • Butter - 250g

  • Vanilla extract - 1 teaspoon

  • Eggs - 2

  • Chocolate chips - 300g

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 170°C and line a baking sheet with parchment paper .

  2. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt.

  3. In another bowl, cream the butter, sugar, brown sugar and vanilla extract with a fork.

  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing after each.

  5. Gradually beat in flour mixture until it's uniform.

  6. Add chocolate chips.

  7. Form small balls of dough and place on the prepared baking sheet.

  8. Bake for 9 - 11 minutes, until golden brown

  9. Store in an airtight container.

Enjoy!
